Fettuccini Alfredo

The classic fettuccini alfredo

Bla Bla. Fettuccini Alfredo stuff.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine noodles
  • 1/2 Cup of unsalted butter
  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 cups of Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 2 tbsp of fresh Italian parsley, chopped
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
